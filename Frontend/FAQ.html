Getting Started

ChopSmo is a comprehensive meal planning and recipe suggestion platform designed to simplify your cooking experience. We help you discover new recipes, plan your meals for the week, and make cooking more enjoyable and organized.

Getting started is easy! Simply:

  • Create a free account by clicking "Sign Up"
  • Set up your dietary preferences and restrictions
  • Start exploring our recipe collection
  • Create your first meal plan

Yes! ChopSmo offers a free tier that includes access to our basic recipe collection, meal planning tools, and essential features. We also offer premium plans with additional features for users who want enhanced functionality.

Meal Planning

Our meal planning feature allows you to organize your meals for the week. You can browse our recipe collection, add meals to specific days, and generate shopping lists automatically based on your planned meals.

Absolutely! You can fully customize your meal plans by adding, removing, or swapping recipes. You can also set dietary preferences, allergies, and portion sizes to get personalized recommendations.

Recipes

We currently have over 5,000 recipes in our database, with new recipes being added regularly. Our collection includes diverse cuisines, dietary options, and difficulty levels to suit every cook.

Yes! You can save recipes to your favorites list by clicking the heart icon on any recipe. Your saved recipes are easily accessible from your profile for quick meal planning.

Account Management

You can update your dietary preferences anytime by going to your Profile settings. Here you can specify allergies, dietary restrictions, preferred cuisines, and cooking skill level.

Yes, you can delete your account at any time through your Profile settings. Please note that this action is irreversible and will permanently remove all your saved recipes and meal plans.

Technical Support

ChopSmo works best on modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend keeping your browser updated for the best experience.

Currently, ChopSmo is a web-based platform optimized for both desktop and mobile browsers. We're working on dedicated mobile apps that will be available soon!
